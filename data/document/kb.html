Subscription Status Change

A subscription goes through various states. These include:

1.Active: When you approve a subscription, it goes to an active state. The customer can use the subscription.

2.Cancelled: When you cancel a subscription, it goes to cancelled state and cannot be reactivated.

3.Pending: The subscription is pending until the validation is complete. You can either approve or reject the subscription.

4.Suspended: The subscription is paused temporarily and can be reactivated later.

5.Terminated: The subscription can no longer be used and cannot be reactivated.
